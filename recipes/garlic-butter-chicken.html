Garlic Butter Chicken

What you'll need

It is time to spoil your taste buds with these amazing chicken thighs. The recipe is easy to make and only takes about 20 minutes of your day

Ingredients for prepping the chicken

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 1 generous tsp of (seasoning) salt
  • 0.5 tbsp of smoked paprika
  • 1 small tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • As much garlic powder as your belly desires

Ingredients for cooking the chicken

  • Olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • Fresh thyme
  • As much butter as you need

How to make

  1. Mix the spices together
  2. Set a few pinches aside and use the rest to marinate the chicken
  3. Let the chicken rest. If you are patient unlike me, let it marinate in the fridge overnight
  4. Heat up the pan with oil and sear the chicken for a few minutes on high heat
  5. Lower the heat, add the butter, leftover of the spices, garlic cloves and thyme
  6. Bath the chicken in the butter with a spoon until it's done.
